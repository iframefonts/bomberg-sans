Bomberg Sans is a proportional sans-serif typeface variant based on iframe fonts's fixed-width Bomberg Serif family (2023). Originally designed by George Chalkidis in 2023.

Bomberg Sans includes Latin Vietnamese and all Western, Central, and South-Eastern European language support.

To contribute, see github.io/iframefonts/bomberg-sans.
